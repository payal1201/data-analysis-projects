# AEROFIT

## About AeroFit:

  AeroFit is a fitness equipment company specializing in treadmills designed to meet diverse customer needs. The company aims to enhance its marketing strategy by   understanding customer demographics and preferences for each treadmill model to improve product targeting and recommendations.

## Data Source - 

  https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/001/125/original/aerofit_treadmill.csv?1639992749

## Project Objective:
To perform descriptive analytics and develop detailed customer profiles for each AeroFit treadmill model by analyzing demographic and behavioral data.

  ### Key Goal — 
    The company wants to identify:

    Which customer characteristics (age, gender, marital status, income) influence treadmill choice.

    How customer preferences differ across treadmill models to enable better product recommendations and personalized marketing.

## Insights:

  KP281 is the most popular model across all customer segments, appealing broadly to both genders and marital groups.

  KP781 shows a strong male preference, indicating a need for marketing adjustments to attract more female buyers.

  KP481 appeals evenly to both men and women, making it ideal for couples or family marketing campaigns.

  Younger customers prefer compact and affordable models, while older or higher-income groups prioritize durability and performance.

## Recommendations:

  Position KP281 as the flagship model in general marketing and digital campaigns.

  Rebrand or tailor promotions for KP781 to increase engagement among women.

  Market KP481 as the “shared fitness” option for families or partners.

  Implement personalized marketing strategies based on age, fitness goals, and lifestyle to improve conversion rates.

  Use insights from customer profiles to guide product placement, promotional focus, and future treadmill design.
