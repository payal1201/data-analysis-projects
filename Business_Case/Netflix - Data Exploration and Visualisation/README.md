# NETFLIX

## About Netflix:
  Netflix is a global leader in media and video streaming with over 222 million subscribers and 10,000+ movies and TV shows worldwide. The dataset provides detailed information on all titles, including cast, directors, ratings, release year, duration, and genre.

## Data Source - 

https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/000/940/original/netflix.csv

## Project Objective:
  To analyze Netflix’s content catalog and derive actionable insights to guide strategic decisions on content production, audience targeting, and regional growth.

  ### Key Goal - 
    The company wants to identify:

    Which content types, genres, and ratings attract the most viewers.

    How production trends vary by country and year.

    What opportunities exist to optimize content strategy and regional market expansion.

## Insights & Recommendations:

  Content Mix: Movies dominate 72% of the catalog, suggesting a strong audience preference — Netflix should continue prioritizing movie production while maintaining selective TV show expansion.

  Regional Focus: The U.S. leads content creation (≈30%), followed by India and the U.K.; expanding regional and local-language content in emerging markets can drive new subscriptions.

  Audience Targeting: Mature (TV-MA) and young-adult content (TV-14, PG) make up the majority, showing a high demand for both adult and family-friendly genres.

  Genre Popularity: Drama is the most dominant genre (≈15%), followed by comedy and action, indicating where future investments can yield engagement.

  Trend Insights: Movie production peaked in 2018, while TV shows surged from 2016–2020, especially during the pandemic period.

## Recommendations:

  Focus on high-quality movies and select TV originals with strong audience appeal.

  Expand regional storytelling — invest in Indian and multilingual productions to attract local subscribers.

  Balance mature content with family-oriented programming to widen audience reach.

  Leverage data-driven recommendations to refresh older content and enhance engagement.

Continue experimenting with interactive formats and unique storylines to maintain a competitive edge.
