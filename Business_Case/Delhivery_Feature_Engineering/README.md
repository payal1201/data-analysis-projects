# Delhivery Logistics Analysis

## About Delhivery
  Delhivery is India’s largest and fastest-growing fully integrated logistics and supply chain company by revenue (Fiscal 2021). The company aims to build the           “operating system for commerce” by combining world-class infrastructure, efficient logistics operations, and cutting-edge technology.

  The data team at Delhivery leverages vast delivery and transaction data to enhance efficiency, quality, and profitability.

## Project Objective
  To analyze and process logistics data from Delhivery’s data engineering pipelines to extract meaningful business insights.
  ### Key goals:
  Clean and transform raw data into useful analytical features.
  Derive actionable insights to improve delivery performance and customer satisfaction.
  Support data science teams in building accurate forecasting models.

## Insights & Recommendations
  Through extensive feature engineering, data processing, and exploratory analysis, we uncovered key regional and product-level patterns influencing business performance.
  Maharashtra and Haryana emerged as high-potential markets, suggesting strategic focus for quick revenue wins.
  Gurgaon showed the strongest sales growth, ideal for targeted offers and loyalty programs.
  Promoting top-selling products in leading states can significantly boost profits.
  Improving delivery performance and follow-ups can increase repeat orders and customer retention.
  Expanding into Tier-2 cities and building local partnerships can drive long-term growth.

  By transforming complex datasets into actionable insights, this analysis enables Delhivery to make informed strategic decisions, optimize logistics operations, and achieve sustainable growth.
